-   ***Thyroglossal duct cyst***

    -   Midline cyst

    -   LM:

        -   lined by flattened, respiratory type or squamous epithelium.

        -   Cysta may have thyroid tissue within wall.

-   ***Heterotopic thyroid***

    -   ***Lingual thyroid***

        -   Normal thyroid tissue present at base of tongue.
